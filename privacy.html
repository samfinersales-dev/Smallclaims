Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 29, 2026

SmallClaimsHelper helps people generate court documents for small claims cases. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have. We do not sell your data. We collect only what we need to deliver the service.

1. What this policy covers

This Privacy Policy applies to smallclaimsforms.net and the SmallClaimsHelper service ("we," "us," "SmallClaimsHelper"). It describes how we handle information from visitors and customers ("you").

2. Information we collect

Information you provide

  • Case information. When you use the document generator, you provide your name and address (as plaintiff), the defendant's name and address, the dispute type, dollar amount, dates, narrative description, and evidence list. This information is used to generate your court documents.
  • Payment information. Payments are processed by Stripe. We do not see, receive, or store your full credit card number. We receive only a customer ID, the amount paid, your email, and the plan you bought.
  • Communications. If you email us for support, we receive your email address and the contents of your message.

Information collected automatically

  • Basic request data. Our servers receive your IP address, browser user-agent, and the pages you visit. This is used for security and to fix bugs.
  • Cookies. We use a small number of strictly necessary cookies for the payment processor. We do not use third-party advertising trackers.

3. How we use your information

  • To generate your small claims documents from the answers you provide.
  • To process payments and send receipts.
  • To prevent abuse and protect the service (such as rate limits and fraud detection).
  • To respond to your support requests.
  • To comply with legal obligations.

4. Who we share information with

We share data only with service providers we use to run SmallClaimsHelper. We do not sell your information.

  • Anthropic — operator of the AI model that drafts your documents. The case information you enter is sent to Anthropic's API to generate the documents. Anthropic does not use your data to train its models when accessed through the API.
  • Stripe — our payment processor.
  • Netlify — our hosting provider.
  • Legal compliance. We may disclose information if required by law, valid court order, or to protect the rights and safety of users or the public.

5. About defendant information

To draft a complaint, the document generator asks you for the defendant's name and address. We process that information solely to produce the documents. We do not retain a separate record of defendants. The completed documents are not stored on our servers — they are returned to your browser and we do not keep a copy linked to your identity.

6. How long we keep your data

  • Your case answers and generated documents are not retained on our servers after your session ends.
  • Payment records are retained as required by tax and accounting law.
  • If you contact support, we retain support correspondence for up to 2 years to handle follow-ups.

7. Security

We use industry-standard practices: HTTPS for all traffic, role-restricted access to service providers, and encrypted storage at our payment processor. No system is perfectly secure, but we work to keep your information safe.

8. Your rights and choices

  • Access and deletion. Email support@smallclaimsforms.net to request a copy of your data or to request deletion.
  • California (CCPA/CPRA), EU/UK (GDPR), and similar laws. Depending on where you live, you may have additional rights to access, correct, delete, or limit processing of your personal information. Contact us to exercise these rights. We do not sell personal information.

9. Not legal advice

SmallClaimsHelper generates template court documents based on your inputs. We are not a law firm. The documents we generate are not legal advice. Small claims procedures vary by state and county. Always verify the correct forms and filing requirements with your local courthouse. For complex matters, consult a licensed attorney in your state.

10. Children

SmallClaimsHelper is not directed at children under 13 and we do not knowingly collect data from them.

11. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy. The "last updated" date at the top reflects the most recent version.

12. Contact

Questions about this policy? Email support@smallclaimsforms.net.
