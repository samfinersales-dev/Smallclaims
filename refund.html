Refund Policy

Effective date: April 30, 2026 · Last updated: April 30, 2026

Quick summary: 30-day money-back guarantee, no questions asked. Email support@smallclaimsforms.net with the email address you used at checkout and we'll process a full refund within 5–10 business days.

30-day money-back guarantee

We want you to be confident in your purchase. If SmallClaimsHelper does not meet your needs for any reason, you can request a full refund within 30 days of the original purchase date.

You do not need to give a reason, but we welcome honest feedback so we can improve the Service.

How to request a refund

Email support@smallclaimsforms.net with:

  • The email address you used at checkout
  • The order/receipt number from your Stripe confirmation email (if you have it)
  • A brief note — optional, but appreciated

We acknowledge refund requests within 2 business days and process approved refunds within 5–10 business days. The funds return to the same card or payment method used at purchase. Your bank may take an additional 3–5 business days to post the refund.

Automatic refund if delivery fails

If our system fails to deliver your purchase within 24 hours of a successful payment, you are entitled to an automatic full refund without filing a request. Contact us at the email above with your receipt and we will process the refund immediately.

What happens after a refund

  • You may no longer use any documents or outputs generated via the Service.
  • Any active subscription is cancelled and will not auto-renew.
  • We delete your stored profile within 30 days, as described in our Privacy Policy.

Exceptions

We may decline refunds (after investigating and communicating with you) in cases of:

  • Evidence of abuse, fraud, or chargeback circumvention (e.g., the same person repeatedly purchasing and refunding);
  • Use of the Service in violation of our Terms of Service;
  • Requests filed more than 30 days after the original charge.

Chargebacks

If you have an issue with your charge, please email us first. A chargeback before we have had a chance to resolve the issue may result in denial of future service, but does not waive your right to a refund under this policy.

Contact

Email: support@smallclaimsforms.net
Typical response: within 2 business days.
